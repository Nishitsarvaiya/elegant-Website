Iceland - The element
of nature

Iceland, a Nordic island nation, is defined by its dramatic landscape with volcanoes, geysers, hot springs and lava fields. Massive glaciers are protected in Vatnajökull and Snæfellsjökull national parks.

Viking Ties

Iceland was settled by Vikings from Norway sometime in the 800s.

Volcanoes

There are more than 125 volcanic mountains in the country.

Glaciers

Glaciers are responsible for carving out everything in Iceland.
